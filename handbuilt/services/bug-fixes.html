Bug Fixes & QA

Clear your bug backlog without pulling engineers off feature work.

The problem

Your bug tracker is growing. Some are customer-reported, some are regressions, some have been open for months. Every bug you assign to an engineer is a feature that doesn't ship. But ignoring them erodes quality and trust.

How we solve it

Our agents systematically work through your bug backlog: reproduce, diagnose root cause, implement the fix, add regression tests, and open a PR. A senior engineer reviews each fix to ensure it doesn't introduce new issues. Your team just reviews and merges.

Common questions

How do you prioritize which bugs to fix?

We work with your existing priority system. If you have P0/P1/P2 labels, we follow them. Otherwise we'll suggest a triage based on customer impact.

What if a bug is hard to reproduce?

Our agents are patient. They'll set up the exact conditions, test under load, check race conditions, and document reproduction steps even for intermittent bugs.

Do you also improve test coverage?

Every bug fix includes a regression test. If we notice gaps in surrounding test coverage, we flag them and can address them as part of the engagement.
